About the Project

Navoiy-Terra v1.0 is the first open-access computational corpus of Alisher Navoi's (1441-1501) literary works, built on Fractal Metascience Paradigm (FMP) principles.

This corpus includes 26 annotated texts (ghazals, rubaiyat, tuyuqs) with 9-language semantic mappings, interactive visualizations, and complete documentation for digital humanities research.
